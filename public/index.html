How it works

The practice run before the real call.

Before a big pitch, a new client, or a difficult renewal — your team dials in, faces the objections, and walks in ready. No guesswork. No surprises.

01
📞

Dial in and practice

Your team calls a dedicated number before their real client call. An AI prospect answers — natural conversation, real pressure, genuine objections. The practice run that prepares them for what's coming.

02
🎯

Handle the objections

The AI throws the objections your team faces every day. "I'm already with someone else." "Your fee is too high." "I need to think about it." They respond live, under real pressure, before it counts.

03
📊

Instant feedback

The moment the call ends, a full scorecard appears — confidence, rebuttal quality, pacing, close attempt. They know exactly what to sharpen before walking into the real thing.
